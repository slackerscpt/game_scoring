Scoring Rules for Uno. 

https://www.officialgamerules.org/uno

There are two scoring options.  

Traditional: First player to 500 wins, gets all points from oppents. 

Alternative: Lowest score wins, players over 500 are elminated. 

First run, will be handling Traditional scoring. 

Second run will be handing Alternative scoring. 

Game play:

1) Determine Number of Players and names
2) Determine scoring path. 
3) Run Game
4) Gather points from players, add points to score. 
5) Announce players scores, and when player wins.
